How Do We Execute A Transcription Project?

You can count on us for quick delivery. We are ISO-certified (ISO 9001:2015, ISO 17100:2015 & ISO/IEC 27001:2013), and our processes ensure that you receive the highest quality output. As a professional transcription services agency, we help you set up the best audio recording equipment. Remember, good quality audio means you’ll not lose important details.

Some projects require simple transcriptions to be done while others get tricky with the timestamps, timecode, languages and much more. The trick here then is to clearly understand what is the expected outcome from the transcription exercise.

Once we understand the scope of work, then we plan project execution after considering the expected date of delivery, and then finalise timelines.

After confirmation on the delivery timelines, the project is then sent for execution to the respective transcribers and the project managers ensure that the projects are being executed as per the timelines.

We have a 3 step quality check process . Once the team internally receives the transcribed document, it is first sent to a professional linguist for checking the quality, then it is sent back to the original transcriber for revisions and rechecks, and finally the document is reviewed internally for formatting or duplicity errors.

The final transcribed files are delivered to the customers along with the transcription certificate to prove that the document is transcribed to the best of the organisation’s abilities and a warranty is given using which the customers (if unsatisfied) can instruct the project managers for the amendments to be made in the transcribed documents for upto 2 times.
